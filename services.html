Virtual Decor Services

  • Design consultation via email or phone/FaceTime
  • Client provides images and room measurements of spaces they would like designed
  • Suggestions on how to best utilize client's existing furniture and accessories and what to edit or add to compliment their needs and style
  • Auto-Cad floor plan created of client's space with furniture layout suggestions
  • Virtual design board with furniture, paint palette, fabrics, flooring, lighting, art, wallpaper, accessories, etc.
  • Instructions on product selections, pricing and purchasing

For the Home Seller

  • Pre-Staging Consultations (suggesting home improvement projects and tasks)
  • Floor Plan Layouts (maximizing the function and flow of each room)
  • Color Consultations (creating a custom paint palette for the home)
  • Project Management (supervising all aspects of the staging process and labor)
  • Pre-Packing (packing and labeling items removed from the home while on the market)
  • Inventory/Furniture Selection (customizing furniture and accessory package)
  • Inventory Rental (overseeing the rental company contract)
  • Inventory Deliver/Pickup (verifying rental furniture is delivered and picked up promptly)
  • Showcasing (selecting accessories, art, greenery, etc. for the home to look its best)
  • De-Staging (removing accessory package rental items)

For the Home Owner

  • Consultations (suggesting home improvement projects)
  • Floor Plan Layouts (maximizing the function and flow of each room for your lifestyle)
  • Color Consultations (creating a custom paint palette for your home based on your taste, the home's architectural style and the natural light in the home)
  • Project Management (supervising all aspects of the decorating process, labor budget, invoicing, orders, vendor and sub-contractor management, and delivery scheduling)
  • Organizing (sorting, de-cluttering and disposing and/or storing of items chosen for removal)
  • Furniture Selection (customizing furniture and accessory package)
  • Goods Delivery (verifying furniture and accessories are delivered safely)
  • Showcasing (staging accessories, art, greenery, etc. for the home to look its best)
  • Exterior Appeal (improving curb appeal and outdoor living, partnering with landscapers when necessary)

  • Event Auto Cad layout plans & event styling
  • Accessory selection
